Internal Auditing Annual Conference

Online

4-Months

18-sessions

The Annual Internal Audit Conference is a diverse professional platform that brings together leaders, experts, and practitioners from all around the world. The event is not just a gathering, but an interactive learning experience aimed at advancing the internal audit profession, showcasing achievements, and opening a new chapter for institutional excellence. The conference offers a unique opportunity to connect with top decision-makers and experts, explore best global practices, and discover innovative tools and solutions that support governance, compliance, and the ability to tackle future challenges. It also provides an inspiring environment for exchanging ideas, motivating participants, and building a strong professional network that contributes to elevating the profession locally and globally.

Transformation

The Kingdom is witnessing a transformative phase of professional development aligned with global advancements. The Saudi Authority of Internal Auditors is reinforcing its leadership role, enabling wider impact across sectors, and supporting professionals in leading change within their organizations.

Empowerment

Empowering professionals with knowledge and skills, while providing continuous development opportunities for national talent to strengthen their readiness to face challenges and seize future opportunities.

Pioneer

Establishing the Kingdom’s position in internal auditing locally and globally through innovation and insight into future professional trends.

Conference objectives

This conference has six objectives, listed below.

1-  Explore future opportunities and identify emerging trends in the profession

2-  Strengthen professional capabilities to tackle complex challenges

3-  Empower participants to implement global best practices

4-  Foster innovation and creativity in professional and technological practices

5-  Build a professional network that creates new pathways for local and global collaboration

6-  Transform challenges into opportunities

Success Stories Workshops

As part of the 2025 Annual Conference, internal audit departments in the Kingdom will have the opportunity to present their inspiring success stories and highlight their key professional experiences through interactive works hops. These workshops aim to promote knowledge sharing and strengthen the efficiency of internal audit functions. Submitted topics will be reviewed, and the most outstanding ones will be selected for presentation during the workshops. This provides a professional platform to showcase achievements in internal auditing and support the ongoing development of the profession.
