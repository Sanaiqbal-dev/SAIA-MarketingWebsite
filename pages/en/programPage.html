Internal Audit Leadership Program

Online

4-Months

18-sessions

It is a combination of Harvard's latest research and innovations in leadership science, and the latest updates on the internal audit field based on the IIA global standards.

1-  A valid membership of the Saudi Authority of Internal Auditors

2-  A Minimum bachelor’s degree holder

3-  A Minimum 5 years of experience in the field of internal Audit

4-  Passing English Proficiency Test or Provide a previously owned English Proficiency test equivalent to 5.5 in IELTS

5-  Submit the request to the identified engineering office, then complete the information and attachments in the engineering office and send it to the municipality.

1-  Definition of governance and its importance for companies and organizations

2-  Types and principles of governance

3-  Components of governance implementation

4-  Activating governance mechanisms within organizations

5-  Risk management methodology
